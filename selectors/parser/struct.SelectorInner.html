The cores parts of a selector used for matching. This exists to make that information accessibly separately from the specificity and pseudo-element information that lives on |Selector| proper. We may want to refactor things and move that information elsewhere, at which point we could rename this to |Selector|.

Fields

complex: ComplexSelector<Impl>

The selector data.

ancestor_hashes: [u32; 4]

Ancestor hashes for the bloom filter. We precompute these and store them inline to optimize cache performance during selector matching. This matters a lot.
